A new fitness app uses worlds first AI-powered motion tracking technology, without the need for additional hardware, to transform your phone into a virtual personal trainer. the virtual personal trainer guides users through the perfect squat. Additionally, the app tracks the position and movement of 16 key points on the users’ body through the phone’s camera. The app then compares users’ actual pose with an ‘ideal’ pose. Furthermore, it compares metrics, including the relative positions of limbs and joints, and the angles between them. The app offers real-time audio and video feedback and encouragement to help users’ hit their maximum number of squats.

Exerwise puts the world’s smartest personal trainer in your pocket. With just the camera on your phone, it counts your reps, corrects your posture, brings tracking and competition to nearly any exercise, and provides audio workouts personalized to your performance in real time.
